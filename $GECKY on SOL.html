HOW TO BUY

1

Create a wallet

Download the Phantom extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring SOL from an exchange or another wallet.

2

Get some SOL

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet" and select Phantom.

3

Go to Raydium

To swap SOL for GECKY, select SOL in the "from" field and choose GECKY in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4

Swap SOL for Gecky

Verify your transaction status in Phantom. You are the proud owner of $GECKY!
